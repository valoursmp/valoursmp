FAQ

Common questions

Some quick answers. You can add more as questions come up on Discord.

Is this a public server?
No. Valour SMP is a limited-slot, whitelisted Civilization SMP. You must apply through Discord and be accepted to join.
Is it Java-only?
Yes. The server is Java Edition only. Bedrock and cracked clients are not supported.
Are there land claims or /tp?
No. There are no claim systems or teleport commands like /home or /tpa. You move, travel, and defend your territory the hard way.
Will the world ever reset?
The goal is to keep the world continuous for the season, so history is visible. Any major changes (like a new season) will be announced well in advance.
How are civilizations assigned?
You can select a preferred civilization in your application, but final placement may be adjusted for balance between the four major regions.
